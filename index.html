About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Division of Cancer Epidemiology and Genetics, National Cancer Institute. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2013, and my Ph.D. degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018, which also included a year of joint Ph.D. training in the Department of Statistics and Operations Research at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Following that, I was a research fellow in the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering & Management at the National University of Singapore.
